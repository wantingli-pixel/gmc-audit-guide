GMC Audit Tool

Automated feed health checks
in 4 steps

Check your client's product feed against 19+ quality rules.
Get a health score and a breakdown of every issue, so you know exactly what to fix.

Step 1 Export your feed Download from Google Merchant Center and paste into the template.
Step 2 Customise rules Add, edit, or turn off checks in the config tab before running.
Step 3 Run the audit Submit the form with your sheet link. Results ready in 2-3 min.
Step 4 Read your results Open the auto-generated report and fix from the lowest scores first.
Step 1 Export your feed

Get your products into the template

1
Go to Google Merchant Center and navigate to Products > All Products
2
Click Download (top right) and export as a spreadsheet or TSV
3
Open the Product Feed Template and paste your product data in
Open Product Feed Template →
Make a copy of this template for each client. Do not edit the original.
Step 2 Optional but recommended

Customise your audit rules

What you can change

Open the Custom Checks Config tab

Add a check New row, write your rule in the Instruction column in plain English
Edit a check Update the Instruction column with your new rule
Adjust weight Change the Weight column (higher = more impact on health score)
Turn off a check Set the Active column to FALSE
Delete a check Delete the entire row

What you cannot change

Checks highlighted in light blue are hardcoded. You can adjust their weight, but the pass/fail logic is fixed.

Step 4 Read your results

Your report is a new Google Sheet tab 'issue overall', auto-created after the audit finishes

What's in the report

Sheet1 Health score, total products, pass rate per check
Weight Guide Score legend, tier explanations, why each check matters
Issue tabs One tab per failing check — every product that failed, with the specific issue flagged
Start with the lowest-scoring checks in the Overview tab. Each issue tab shows exactly which products to fix.
